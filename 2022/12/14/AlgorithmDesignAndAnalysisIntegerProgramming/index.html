7 Problem

A construction company has contracted to build two types of dormitories. Each dormitory of type A covers an area of si , and each dormitory of type B covers an area of sj . The company has purchased a building land of s. According to the plan, the number of Type A dormitories shall not exceed ni , and the number of Type B dormitories shall not exceed nj . One dormitory of type A can earn ci , and one dormitory of type B can earn cj . How many type A and B dormitories should the company build?

少条件，推测是少了总面积条件，假设总面积为S。

然后题目就可以用二元一次方程做。
